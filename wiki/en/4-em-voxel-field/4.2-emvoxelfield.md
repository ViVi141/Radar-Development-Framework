# EMVoxelField

Sparse spatial electromagnetic energy field with two-level chunked grid.

---

## Key APIs

**Injection**:

- `InjectPowerAt(pos, power, freqMask, dir, ttl)`
- `InjectAlongRay(start, dir, length, power, ...)`
- `InjectReflection(...)`
- `InjectJammer(...)`

**Query**:

- `GetPowerAt(pos, out cell)` — Power at position (trilinear interpolation)
- `GetTopCells(count, out cells)`
- `GetSignalDescriptorAt(pos, out descriptor)`

**Update**:

- `TickDecay(dt)` — Linear decay + TTL expiry

**Sector**:

- `RegisterActiveSector(pos, dir, halfAngleDeg, radius, ttl)` — Cone-shaped active region
- `m_OutOfSectorDecayMul` — Faster decay outside sector
- `PruneToBudget()` — Enforce chunk budget
