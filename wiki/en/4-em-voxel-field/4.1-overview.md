# EM Voxel Field Overview

The EM Voxel Field is a spatial electromagnetic energy field. It provides:

- **EMVoxelField** — Sparse voxel grid with power, frequency mask, direction, TTL
- **EMPassiveSensor** — Passive sensor for sampling field power and signal descriptors
- **Network sync** — Server-authoritative sync via EMFieldNetworkComponent
- **Debug visualization** — EMVoxelDebugVisualizer for power/direction display

---

## Architecture

- **Two-level grid**: 200 m chunks, 10 m voxels (20³ = 8000 voxels per chunk)
- **SOA storage**: Flat parallel arrays in EMChunk
- **Lazy rays**: Zero-write injection; query-time evaluation for passive sensors
- **Trilinear interpolation**: Sub-voxel read precision
- **Morton ordering**: Z-order for cache locality
- **Temporal LoD**: Update priority (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW) controls step size
