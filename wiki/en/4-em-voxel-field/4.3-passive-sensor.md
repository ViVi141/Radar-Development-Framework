# EMPassiveSensor

Passive sensor for sampling the EM voxel field. Detects energy and extracts signal descriptors.

---

## Usage

Attach to an entity. Sample power and descriptors at positions. Detection logic uses power threshold and optional signal descriptor analysis.

---

## Key Methods

- Sample field power at a position
- Extract signal descriptors (waveform type, PRF, pulse width, frequency)
- Detection decision based on threshold and optional rules
