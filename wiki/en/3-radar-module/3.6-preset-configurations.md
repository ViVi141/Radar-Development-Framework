# Radar Preset Configurations

## RDF_RadarDemoConfig Presets

| Factory Method | Band | Typical Use | Max Range |
|----------------|------|-------------|-----------|
| `CreateHelicopterRadar(rayCount=512)` | X (10 GHz) | Helicopter forward sector | 8 000 m |
| `CreateXBandSearch(rayCount=512)` | X (10 GHz) | Ground search/surveillance | 3 000 m |
| `CreateAutomotiveRadar(rayCount=256)` | Ka (77 GHz) | Vehicle FMCW collision | 150 m |
| `CreateWeatherRadar(rayCount=1024)` | S (3 GHz) | Weather detection | 5 000 m |
| `CreatePhasedArrayRadar(rayCount=2048)` | C (5.6 GHz) | Multi-target phased array | 2 000 m |
| `CreateLBandSurveillance(rayCount=512)` | L (1.3 GHz) | Long-range early warning | 5 000 m |

---

## Usage

```c
// Build settings only (manual Scanner creation)
RDF_RadarSettings settings = RDF_RadarDemoConfig.CreateXBandSearch().BuildRadarSettings();

// Build full Scanner (recommended)
RDF_RadarScanner scanner = RDF_RadarDemoConfig.CreateHelicopterRadar().BuildRadarScanner();
```
