# Radar Architecture Overview

The Radar module extends the LiDAR scanning core with full electromagnetic physics: propagation, RCS, Doppler, SNR, and PPI HUD display.

---

## Design Principles

- **Inheritance**: `RDF_RadarScanner` extends `RDF_LidarScanner` and reuses ray tracing.
- **Modularity**: Physics, modes, visualization, and demo logic are isolated.
- **Extensibility**: Custom RCS models, color strategies, radar modes, scan-complete callbacks.

---

## Data Flow

```
RDF_RadarSettings + RDF_EMWaveParameters
       |
       v
RDF_RadarScanner.Scan(entity)
  +-- Inherits Lidar ray Trace flow
  +-- ApplyRadarPhysics(sample) for each hit:
       1. FSPL (RDF_RadarPropagation)
       2. Atmospheric attenuation
       3. Rain attenuation
       4. RCS estimation (RDF_RCSModel)
       5. Radar equation -> received power
       6. Range gate (m_MinRange)
       7. Noise kTBF -> SNR
       8. Doppler shift
       9. Detection threshold
      10. Clutter filter (optional MTI)
       |
       v
RDF_RadarHUD.OnScanComplete()  (PPI HUD)
RDF_RadarWorldMarkerDisplay    (3D pillars)
RDF_RadarTextDisplay           (ASCII map)
```
