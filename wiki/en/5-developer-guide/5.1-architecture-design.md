# Architecture & Design

## Design Principles

- **Opt-in**: Framework is silent by default. Runs only when explicitly enabled; does not affect other mods.
- **Modularity**: Scanning core, physics, visualization, and demo logic are isolated and can be replaced independently.
- **Extensibility**: Stable extension interfaces (sample strategies, color strategies, scan-complete callbacks).
- **Inheritance**: Radar fully extends LiDAR (`RDF_RadarScanner` extends `RDF_LidarScanner`).
